Parker's Perfect Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients Required:

  • 18 ounces pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 package of pepperoni
  • 14 ounces of mozzarella cheese
  • any desired herbs and seasonings (basil and pepper recommended)
  • flour for dough handling

Steps to Prepare:

  1. preheat the oven to 450 degrees
  2. roll out dough into a circular shape on a flat surface
  3. add sauce evenly to the center of the dough, leaving about 2 inches of dough fr the crust
  4. sprinkle cheese over the sauce evenly
  5. add pepperoni and any additional toppings and seasonings
  6. when the oven is ready, place the pzza in the center of your pizza stone and cook for 8 minutes. then turn pizza around in the oven and cook for an additional 8 minutes.
  7. when the pizza looks golden brown on the edges, remove it from the oven and allow it to cool for 10-15 minutes.
  8. slice and serve the pizza!
