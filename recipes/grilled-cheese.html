Grilled Cheese!

Ingredients Required:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of gouda cheese

Steps to Prepare:

  1. spread 1/2 tbsp of butter on one side of each bread slice
  2. place the slices butter-side down in a heated pan
  3. stack the slices of cheese on the bread, and when the bread appears golden brown, put the two pieces together in a sandwich
  4. continue cooking until the bread appears a rich golden brown, continue flipping and pressing lightly on the bread with a spatula for a few minutes as you allow the bread to crisp and cheese to melt.
  5. remove from the pan, allow the sandwich to cool, and cut diagonally in half to serve.
